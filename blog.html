About

In 1996, I was born in Columbia, Missouri in the United States but I grew up in Turkey. I finished my Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at Izmir Institute of Technology. After some work experience that I have got in Istanbul, I decided to relocate to the place that I was born. Now, I'm studying Data Science and Analytics in BioHealth at the University of Missouri-Columbia, and working as a Software Engineer at Cerner. Besides, I love traveling to different countries and learn about their cultures. Furthermore, I always interested in sports and music. I have played basketball in different teams, and I have been playing guitar for 13 years.
